OneNote Preset Colors

I am sometimes a color paranoid. OneNote comes with some preset pens. The very first time I started using Wacom I noticed those certain pen colors and those got stucked in my mind. Among these colors I like the green most. I don't know how much I have tried to reproduce this color- clearing OneNote data, reinstalling OneNote. But those preset didn't come back. I gave up accepting that somethings aren't meant to be achieved. But one day, out of nowhere I got the color back. I again had to struggle to understand the system. The thing is, when a color is selected from the more color options, OneNote generates more color variants from that color and displays them(shown in the second image for green and blue). From those new colors I got the color I wanted. Nowadays I also struggle to reproduce these colors- for this reason this page exists. Note: I discovered today(12 March 2022) the way to produce blue!
