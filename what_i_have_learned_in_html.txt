					rgb(255, 0, 0) ---->Red
					rgb(0, 255, 0) ---->Green
					rgb(0, 0, 255) ---->Blue
					rgb(0, 0, 0) ---->Black
					rgb(255, 255, 255) ---->White

					The alpha parameter is a number between 0.0(fully transparent) to 1.0(not transparent at all)
